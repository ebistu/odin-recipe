Description

This is a recipe for a vegetarian lasagne which utilizes mozzarella instead of a cheesy sauce

Ingrediants

  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 medium chili
  • 1 dl red lentils
  • 1 kg tomatosauce
  • 2 mozzarella
  • lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. mix the tomato sauce stuff up
  2. layer the sheets and pour the sauce on them
  3. finish up with the mozzarella on the top layer
  4. chuck it in a 200 deg celsius oven for about an hour
